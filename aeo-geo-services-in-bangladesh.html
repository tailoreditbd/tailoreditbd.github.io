FAQ about AEO & GEO

Answers to common questions about answer engine optimization, generative engine optimization, and AI search readiness.

AEO means Answer Engine Optimization. It optimizes website content so search engines, voice assistants, and answer-based platforms can provide direct answers from your content.

GEO means Generative Engine Optimization. It makes your brand and content more understandable, trustworthy, and relevant for AI-powered search engines and generative answer platforms.

SEO focuses on ranking web pages in search results, while AEO focuses on providing direct answers to user questions. AEO supports SEO by making content more helpful, structured, and answer-friendly.
